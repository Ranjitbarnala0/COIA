## 🔬 What is COIA-Ω?

COIA-Ω is a complete photonic AI accelerator blueprint designed end-to-end for researchers and chipmakers exploring the future of compute beyond silicon transistors.

> **The core idea:** Replace electronic matrix multiplications with optical interference patterns — achieving the same math at a fraction of the energy cost, at the speed of light.

### Key Highlights

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| **Energy Efficiency** | ≥3× better than NVIDIA H100 |
| **Primary Use Case** | LLM inference & edge AI |
| **Compute Medium** | Photonic (light-based) |
| **Design Time** | ~10 hours with generative AI |
